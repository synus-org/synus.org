About

Hello, my name is Pawel and I have worked in unsustainable software projects far too many times in my career. I would like to see more of the sustainable ones in the future.

The only way I have found is to spread the word. One part of it is to share what I have learned in written word. Here comes the site.

On the site I collect thoughts, methods and techniques that make a software project sustainable in public, to spread the word.
